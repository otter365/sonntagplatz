CI/CD for Product Managers: Getting Your Hands Dirty

This site is deployed through a simple pipeline: VS Code → GitHub → Cloudflare Pages.

Most product work touches CI/CD indirectly—roadmaps, release coordination, incident follow-ups. I wanted the opposite: a setup where publishing is frictionless and fully under my control.

The result is straightforward: every change is versioned, reviewed, and deployed automatically. The distance between a draft and a live URL is now one commit.

I want this section to document practical systems—how they work, why they matter, and how to keep them maintainable.

Future posts will cover the small mechanics that compound: structure, deployment, release hygiene, and the interface between product decisions and engineering reality.
